about me

Born in Indirapuram, raised and educated in Odisha for 9 years, then Gujarat for another 9, now studying in Kerala (i'm pan indian). I remember my first lucid memory is booting a computer my dad had for my elder cousin. Even though I had a computer it didn't have internet. So most of my time was spent playing Road Rash, pitfall, Zuma; games. No one in my family is a programmer or even remotely in tech, so I was never introduced to that world irl. It was when I got internet that my world began developing. But even there, most of my time was spent on platforms like kogama playing online games making friends who lived far away. Then one day I figured that you could actually make your own games on kogama!!. I didn't end up making a front page game, but I remember that excitement of building something I imagine. I made a few buildings, I had the idea planned "gta but kogama" (i didn't have gta and wanted to play it). And I tried and I couldn't do it alone.
